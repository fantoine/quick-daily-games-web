About

Quick Daily Games is an independent mobile app offering a fresh set of puzzles every day — Sudoku 6×6, Nonogram, Queens, and more to come.

The idea is simple: give yourself a short, satisfying mental challenge each morning. No endless levels, no timers you didn't ask for — just one good puzzle per game, renewed daily for everyone at the same time.

The app is developed and maintained by Fabien Antoine.
