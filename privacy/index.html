Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Quick Daily Games ("the App") is developed and operated by Fabien Antoine. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Data We Collect

The App collects minimal data necessary for its operation:

  • Anonymous user identifier: The App uses Firebase Authentication to assign each device a random anonymous ID. This ID is not linked to your name, email address, or any other personal information.
  • Usage events: Firebase Analytics collects anonymous events (e.g., puzzle started, puzzle completed, time taken) to help us understand how the App is used and improve it. No personally identifiable information is included in these events.
  • Device information: Standard technical information such as device type, operating system version, and app version, collected automatically by Firebase Analytics.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Photos, contacts, or other device content
  • Payment information

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To serve the daily puzzle for your device
  • To compute and display your ranking on the global leaderboard (using your anonymous ID only)
  • To analyze aggregate usage patterns and improve the App
  • To diagnose crashes and technical issues via Firebase Crashlytics

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — Authentication, database, analytics, and crash reporting. Firebase Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

Anonymous usage data is retained according to Firebase's default retention settings (up to 14 months for Analytics data). Your anonymous user ID persists on your device until you uninstall the App or reset it via the App settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be shared with Firebase (Google LLC) solely to operate the services described above.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. The App does not require account creation and does not collect personal information.

8. Your Rights

Since we only collect anonymous data not linked to your identity, we are unable to identify you as an individual. If you wish to reset your anonymous ID, you can do so from the App settings (Settings → Reset all data).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: apps@fantoine.com
